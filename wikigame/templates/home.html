Wikipedia game

You start on a given start article (e.g. Albert Einstein), and your objetive is to reach a target article (e.g. Michael Jackson).

For each article, the game gives you all the links that article contains to other articles. You jump to other articles by following links, just like navigating on the internet.

The game ends when you reach the target article, and your score is them compared against all others players:

wins the player with the shortest path (least number of clicks)

We selected different challenges from which you can start from:

  • Albert Einstein to Michael Jackson

This is a game to understand the idea of a network, and how it can be a model of different physical and social systems.

In this game, you are to navigate on the network of wikipedia articles. The nodes of the network are articles of the wikipedia and the edges are links between articles.
